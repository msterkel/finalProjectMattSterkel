Mozart

  • Lived (1756-1791). Born in Salzburg, Austria.
  • Composed music by age 5.
  • Toured Europe performing and composing as a child.
  • Wrote world famous works like: k.545, the Marrige of Figaro, and Lacrismo.
  • Changed the world of opera, and found huge success in piano sonatas.
  • Considered one of the brightest composers of the classical period.
