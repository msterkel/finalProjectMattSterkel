Ludwig Van Beethoven

  • Lived (1770-1827). Born in Bonn,Germany.
  • First public performance at the age of seven and a half years old.
  • Turbulent family life growing up.
  • Wrote world famous works like: Fur Elise, Moonlight Sonata, and his 5th symphony.
  • Became almost 100% deaf by 1823.
  • Ground breaking music ushered the way for the next period of music, the Romantic period.
